Devops

DevOps is basically a culture. You can say software engineering culture. Aim or objective of DevOps is unifying software development (Dev) and software operation (Ops). DevOps is a broad area and it involves many tools at different stages/phases. This culture minimizes the gap between developers and business operations by providing collaboration layers to both. Developers in DevOps want continuous innovation and product enrichment whereas Ops department oversees costing and delivery.

AWS
